Help

How the swap shop works

Pick your slot, choose the shifts you want off, and the swap shop suggests colleagues who can take them — ideally giving you an equivalent shift in return so the swap is fair.

The basics

Swaps are like-for-like (Day with Day, Night with Night, Ward with Ward, E with E), and the rest days either side travel with the shift so nobody loses out on rest.

If no direct two-way swap is possible, the shop will suggest a three-way swap as a last resort, where everyone still ends up doing the same number of on-calls.

Mutual swaps

When you click “Find a swap”, the dates you want off get added to a shared “looking” list. Anyone else searching for the same dates sees you as a top match. The next time you open the app, you'll see a banner if a colleague is currently looking for one of your shifts — tap it to swap straight away.

Your “looking” status updates each time you search. If you change your mind, hit “Stop looking” in step 2 to clear it.

Your availability

Flag dates you can’t take a swap onto in step 3. These save automatically and are visible to anyone else using the shop, so they won’t suggest you on those days. Edit them whenever your plans change.

Your PIN

Each slot has its own 4-digit PIN. The first time you pick your slot, you set the PIN; on this device you'll stay logged in. If you switch device or browser, enter the same PIN to confirm it's you. Forgot it? Your rota team can reset it.

Picking multiple blocks

When you select blocks across different weeks (say a Day on one week and Nights on another), the shop ranks partners for each block independently. People usually swap one block at a time with whoever fits — you don't need a single person to take everything.

Drafting the emails

Once a match looks good, use “Draft ask-them email” to message the other doctor — agreement usually happens offline. Once they’ve agreed, use “Draft rota-team email” to send the formal swap request. Each email opens in a draft you can edit before sending.

Always check with the rota team. The shop suggests options based on the spreadsheet — the rota team makes every swap official. If anything looks wrong, or you can’t find a sensible swap, speak to them directly.
